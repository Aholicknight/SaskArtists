Welcome to the homepage for
Alexander James Crease

Here you will find all the information available of this Saskatchewan artist, view some of his works, and contact him on your thoughts about his work.

The Bio section is where you can find a concise biography about Alex, and a list of some of his works.

The Gallery page is where you can find a description and digital copy of all of Alex's works.

Finally, the contact section is where you will be given the contact information needed to reply to Alex's Works.
