Relief Work

The first piece of relief work that Pete ever did was carved with hand carving tools. Since then, he has started using an electric hand rouan electric hand router. In his relief work, his use of line is used more to define shape than form.

"Human Works"

This is one of Pete's biggest pieces; he normally does not work to this scale. This piece is full of his symbolism: the bug that represents society, the snake that is a phallic symbol and thesents society, the snake that is a phallic symbol and the man.
