Bio

In 1947, Jack Cowin was born in the city of Indianapolis, Indiana. At age 69, his life has been full of different experiences and works and was mostly spent in Regina. He studied printmaking at the University of Indiana and obtained his Bachelor of Fine Arts in 1969. He then completed his master of fine arts degree two years later from the University of Illinois. He then made his way to Regina, Saskatchewan where he taught printmaking and became a university professor for almost 40 years before retiring in 2007. He later died in September of 2014 in Arlington Heights, Illinois.
