    • Biography

    I was born on this planet earth (Lat: 50 deg. 27min. N by Long: 104 deg. 27min. West) in a Provincial capital known as Regina at a time when the majority believed The Big Lie. St. Cecilia's Torch was lit thrice for me by: Laurence Ritchey (Bell Ringing), McGregor Hone (Visual Arts), and Jeae Ritchey (Bell Ringing), McGregor Hone (Visual Arts), and Jean Oser (Collaborative Arts 'Film'). A Severe Addiction To Technological Change led me to a life as an engineer struggling with Golden Handcuffs. I see the globe as an Evolution Of Tribal Struggle. My art documents my world of Camouflaged Ritual, Paradox With Humour and the Purification Of Violence (although some argue this is only change). As a multifaceted realist I seek wisdom through action, dialogue and exposition. It is thus as society denies me sanctity, I push knowledge (self or universal) to a state of Sanctity Denied. What Is This Man

    I also like to throw stuff off of buildings for Y2K
