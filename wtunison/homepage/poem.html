What Is This Man

By Maxine Athene


What is this man who keeps his breath,

In keeping with the tales that bind him so?

He is a master,

He is a martyr,

Choice a maker,

Last a taker,

He finds himself in spider's snare to save himself,

Whoa be thine!

I will bring you wine,

I will bring you heather,

I will find the solitaire and give it as a gift for your blessing,

Please the sail that carries you,

Appease the wail that covers you,

Be a breaker for the craft that marks your way,

And all the angels will call you destine.
