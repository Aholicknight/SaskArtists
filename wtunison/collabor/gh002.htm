My Golden Handcuffs

My Golden Handcuffs weight 12 or more tons
The Gold is just simple bronze
Yet from hollow bow, the sounds to my ear
Make the world of joy and fun
By Great ropes we are tied to clapper and heart
Sentimental, passion and art
High pitched treble/tenor low, a strike to lip for the public to hear
In this world with these bells I must play my part.

"THE" Collaborative Process
(September 96 - April 97)
