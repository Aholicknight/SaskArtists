I personally feel that society and individuals in our society do not want me to ever reach a state of inner peace. They watch my spending. Why did you spend $12.81 at the IGA? They consider how bright the lights are in the hallway. Is it safe in those dark corridors?

Inner peace is achieved by following one or more prescribed paths of good living. On such a path is the fullfillment of a pact with the supreme deity as a good person. This often requires a display of prudence, self sacrifice, regular prayer and adherance to a code of conherance to a code of conduct. Our environment creates conflict with such life styles. Sometimes there is supression of rituals, other times food laws become difficult to uphold, and still other times the ability to avoid the taboo or unacceptable is impossible.
