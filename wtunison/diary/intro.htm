January 9, 1998

Dr. Petty,

This is the start of my diary thoughts, readings and some of any resulting inspirations (creative embryos). I am writing in the first person to keep this exploratioo keep this exploration separate from my thesis writing. I hope you will comment heavily and at the end we can even consider this an experiment in collaboration and HTML.

Since I believe that this exploration will be non-linear, I propose the following cross-reference indexing system: chronologically, by author, and by subject. This will facilitate reflection. I am also enhancing the submissions with a few oeflection. I am also enhancing the submissions with a few of my images (drawing, painting, and photos (sometimes video still)) as they are finished.
