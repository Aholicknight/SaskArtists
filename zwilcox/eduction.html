Zane Wilcox

Eduction

Zane is a man of art, yet infuence by science and technology. He first completed a Culinary Fine Arts Diploma and a Bachelor of Music in composition in British Columbia. Later on in his life, he shifted to the field of engineering. After, he collected a Ceramic Arts Diploma in Prince Albert. He was apprenticed by Charley Ferrero and influenced by Welsh ceramist Walter Keeler.
