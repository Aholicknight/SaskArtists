A METHOD TO THE MADNESS

    Something must make sense in all this madness.
    Throughout the ages, as always it is great art
    that binds the fabric of the universe.
      The door to our souls
    is swung open by the seemingly inanimate
    we are invited to examine the essence of life itself
      It is in this context that the art of Scott McLeod
      permeates the senses.
      the unseen dominates in this world
      fear is conquered in this domain
      Waves of infinite patience
      pound the shores of this island
    A master rules the hand of this artist
    sprung from manure of the twentieth century
    fertile soil, for those whose toil it is to
    reawahose toil it is to
    reawaken man kind's sleeping soul

Barry Larsen
