Baby photos of Martha Cole.

Martha Cole was born in 1946, in Regina, Saskatchewan.

Most of Martha's art is done with mostly fabrics.

Most of Martha's inspiration comes from nature, community, and spirituality, having a lot of detailed works of trees and leaves, and many works with elevators.

Martha decided that she was going to be an artist at the age of 14. She then went into the field of fine arts in sculpture.

She started by making giant sculptures using metals and welding them together.

She realised that her projects were too large scaled and expensive while living in an apartement, so she started working with fabrics using a normal sewing machine, making her first work quite difficult.
