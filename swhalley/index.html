"The waste that is genarated in creating and developing a city can be more effectively managed. I don't mean civically. I mean individually."

Sean Woodruff Whalley is an artist who pursues the "old-world technologies" of blacksmithing and coopering. Taking discarded pieces of lumber and turning them into art, Sean removes the waste product and makes it into something that everyone can enjoy.
